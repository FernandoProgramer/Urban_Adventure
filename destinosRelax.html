Santorini, Greece
This Greek island is famous for its stunning views of the sea, its picturesque white houses and its impressive sunset. Enjoy a relaxing day at the beach, try the delicious Greek cuisine and walk the beautiful trails of the island.

The cost varies between $2,500 and $4,000 per person.

INCLUDED
-Transfers from the airport to the hotel.
-Accommodation in a boutique hotel with sea views.
-Meals included, with a focus on Mediterranean cuisine.
-Excursions to traditional Santorini villages and local vineyards.
-Yoga sessions at sunrise or sunset.
-Free time to enjoy the panoramic views and sunsets.
-Spa treatments and relaxing massages.
Kyoto, Japan
La antigua capital de Japón es famosa por sus templos, jardines zen y tradiciones culturales. Pasea por sus hermosos jardines, visita los templos históricos y sumérgete en la serenidad de la cultura japonesa.

The cost varies between US $2,000 and US $3,000 per person.

INCLUDED
-Transfers from the airport to the ryokan (traditional Japanese inn).
-Accommodation in a traditional room with tatami mats and a view of a zen garden.
-Kaiseki breakfast and dinner (exquisite Japanese cuisine) included.
-Guided visit to temples and historical gardens.
-Meditation sessions and Japanese calligraphy practices.
-Free time to wander through the quiet alleys and enjoy the serene beauty of Kyoto.
-Experience in an onsen (Japanese thermal bath) to relax body and mind.
Amalfi Coast, Italy
This stunning coastline in southern Italy offers dreamlike scenery, picturesque villages and a tranquil atmosphere. Enjoy the coastal scenery, explore charming villages such as Positano and Ravello, and sample delicious Italian cuisine.

Approximate cost $3,000 to $4,500 per person

INCLUDED
- Transfers from the airport to the hotel.
- Accommodation in a boutique hotel with sea views.
-Meal plan included, with local specialties and fresh products.
-Boat trips along the coast and visits to the picturesque villages.
-Italian cooking classes and local wine tasting.
-Free time to enjoy the quiet beaches and relax on the coast.
-Spa treatments and relaxation massages.
