The Colca Canyon in Peru
It is one of the deepest canyons in the world, located in the Arequipa region of Peru. It offers stunning views of the Peruvian Andes, and is a popular spot for hiking and spotting condors in flight.

Total cost approximately $1,500 to $2,000 dollars

INCLUDED
- Transportation from the hotel in Arequipa to the Colca Canyon.
- Tour guide in Spanish or English.
- Accommodation in a hotel in the Colca Canyon for 2 nights.
- All meals during the stay in the Colca Canyon.
-Excursions to the Cóndor viewpoint, traditional towns and natural hot springs.
-Travel insurance.
The Grand Canyon of Colorado in the United States
It is one of the most famous canyons in the world, located in the state of Arizona in the United States. It is an impressive place for hiking, rafting, climbing and other extreme sports, offering spectacular views of the desert landscape.

Total cost per person: between $1,500 and $2,500 USD.

INCLUDED
-Transfer from the Phoenix airport to the Grand Canyon.
-Hotel accommodation in the Grand Canyon for 3 nights.
-All meals during the stay in the Grand Canyon.
-Extreme sports activities such as rafting and rock climbing.
-Travel insurance.
Easter Island in Chile
It is an island located in the Pacific Ocean, famous for its huge stone statues called "Moai". The island is a place of great archaeological interest, and is also known for its beautiful beaches and extreme water activities such as surfing and diving.
